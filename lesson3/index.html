Site Name:

Beautiful Sunny Day

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the site will be to obtain and display accurate weather information for the user. In addition to this, I will also integrate a type of comment section where users will be able to share information, chat, and network with others in the local area. This site will serve as a hub for all kinds of information for the city.The site will also consist of occasional news articles written by Journalists, and then republished by the site developer.
